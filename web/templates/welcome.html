Unlock Your Full History

One toggle gives Nomolo read access to your Messages, Calendar, Notes, Mail, Photos, and Safari data. Nothing leaves your machine.

1

Open System Settings

Click the button below — it opens the right page directly.

Open Privacy Settings →

Or: System Settings → Privacy & Security → Full Disk Access

2

Find Terminal (or your browser)

Look for Terminal in the list. If it's not there, click the + button and add it from Applications → Utilities.

3

Flip the toggle

Turn on the switch next to Terminal. macOS will ask for your password — that's normal.

Heads up: macOS will close Terminal when you flip this toggle. That's expected.

4

Reopen Terminal & come back

Open Terminal again and run the same command you used to start Nomolo. Open localhost:8000/welcome in your browser — we'll automatically pick up where you left off and scan your newly unlocked sources.
