## The Wedge: A Gamified Data Vault

We make reclaiming your data an adventure, not a compliance exercise. Nomolo is a frictionless, local data vault. Starting with a 1-click Gmail import, it turns your scattered history into a unified, locally owned knowledge graph.

Friction is the enemy; fun over compliance.

## Why Now? The Convergence

Three forces are converging simultaneously:

**Point 1: Model Intelligence** — Open-source and frontier models have solved the reasoning layer. Intelligence is no longer the bottleneck.

**Point 2: Edge Compute** — Hardware like the Mac Mini or NVIDIA DGX Spark brings 100 TFLOPS and 128GB of memory directly to your desk. Zero marginal cost inference.

**Point 3: Data Rights** — GDPR, CCPA, and platform export mandates mean your data is legally yours to extract. The missing piece is the infrastructure to catch it.
